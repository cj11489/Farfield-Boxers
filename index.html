Welcome to Farfield Boxers

"The Farfield affix was established by my late father Les Whitfield in 1955. In the early 1990’s my Dad and I shared the Farfield affix in partnership and I am very proud to be able to continue his legacy. For over 60 years our kennel has strived to own and breed dogs that possess type, soundness, quality and character, and we are proud to have produced champions and ticket winners along the way! We are a small and select kennel and currently have the pleasure of campaigning Panabolo Strike a Deal with Farfield ‘Max’ who continues to fly the flag for us in the show ring with great success. Max lives with us at home in Brentwood, Essex, along with his son, Marley."

James Whitfield
